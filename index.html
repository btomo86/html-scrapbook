Block Elements

Some elements will appear to start on a new line in the browser window, these are known as block level elements
Examples of block elements: < h1> < p> and < li>

Inline Elements

Some elements will always appear to continue on the same line as their neighbouring elements. these are known as inline statements
Examples of inline elements: < a> < b> < em> and < img>

Example of an ordered list (How to make a brew like a proper Yorkshireman)

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aertated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Oxygen in water helps flavour!
  2. Pop a tea bag into a mug, pour over hot water and stir briefly
  3. Tea needs time to unlock all its flavour, so give it 4-5 minutes to do its thing. This is perfect time to munch a sneaky biscuit or daydream about holidays
  4. Before removing the tea bag, gently squidge it against the side of the mug. Just the once.
  5. I like a splash of semi-skimmed milk, but your brew is unique to you- so add milk, sugar, honey or nothing at all. Most importantly, enjoy!
